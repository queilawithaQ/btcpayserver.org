Funda kabanzi   Nikela  

Hola iseva ngefu, eyakho i-hardware noma usebenzise umnikazi okhona.

Xhuma isitolo sakho se-e-commerce noma usebenzise enye yezinhlelo zokusebenza eziningi ukuze ukhokhelwe.

Izimali zifika ngqo ku-wallet yakho exhunyiwe.

  • Izinhlelo zokusebenza ezibalulekile ezakhelwe ngaphakathi

    Izici ozisebenzisa kakhulu zibhakwa kuzo zonke izikhathi ukuze ushaye phansi kugijima.

    Dala uhlelo lokusebenza lwe-Point-of-sale esitolo sakho, shumeka kalula inkinobho yokufaka kuwebhusayithi yakho, thumela isixhumanisi sokukhokha kuklayenti kuwo wonke umhlaba, noma uqalise umkhankaso wokubuyisa imali yokuphakamisa imali ngembangela yakho

    Funda kabanzi  

  • Kuhle kakhulu ku-invoning kanye ne-accounting

    Khiqiza futhi uphathe ama-invoice kalula.

    Yaziswa uma sezikhokhelwa. Khipha ama-invoice akho ngefomethi ehlukene ukuze kube nokugcinwa okubushelelezi.

    Funda kabanzi  

  • Ukuphathwa Kwama-Wallet Omdabu

    Phatha imali yakho engenayo naphumayo nge-wallet eyakhelwe ngaphakathi enokusekelwa kwe-wallet ye-Hardware.

  • Zenzele noma usebenzise iseva eyabiwe

    Sebenzisa iseva eyabiwe uma ungeyona i-tech savvy, noma dlulisa isibonelo sakho kumaseva wamafu noma amadivayisi wehadiwe.

    Ngokwabelana neseva yakho ungasekela abangane noma umphakathi wangakini.
